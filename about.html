About Music Instruments

Music Instruments is a family-owned business that has been providing high-quality instruments to musicians of all levels since 1985. Our mission is to help musicians find the perfect instrument for their needs and budget.

Our History

Music Instruments was founded in 1985 by John Smith, a passionate musician who wanted to help others find the perfect instrument. What started as a small shop has grown into a thriving business with multiple locations across the country.

We take pride in our knowledgeable staff, who are all musicians themselves and are dedicated to helping our customers find the perfect instrument. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned professional, we have the expertise to help you find what you're looking for.

Our Values

  • Quality: We only sell high-quality instruments from reputable brands.
  • Customer Service: We believe in providing exceptional customer service and strive to make every interaction a positive experience.
  • Expertise: Our staff are all musicians themselves and have the knowledge and experience to help you find the perfect instrument.
  • Community: We are proud to be a part of the music community and support local musicians and music education programs.
